## **🧠 Engineering Philosophy**
The principles that guide the system’s design and evolution:

- Explicitness over convention  
- Reproducibility  
- Observability  
- Separation of concerns  
- Small, focused components  
- Continuous learning  
- Building systems that tell the truth
